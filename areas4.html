Welcome to Sport Sheffield

At Goodwin Sports Centre we have a number of great facilities available for general use by our staff and students. Facilities with online accessibility can be booked in advance through your Sport Sheffield account using your muse account details. Bouldering and Swimming can be accessed via our main reception at Goodwin Sports Centre.Please read and click the icons below for additional information about each area. We look forward to seeing you.

We aim to provide the best possible products and services within agreed key sporting themes, in order to support and deliver an outstanding sporting experience.
