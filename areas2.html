JobShop

click on the image to scroll

Welcome to Student's Union JobShop!

In here you will be able to get a valuable advice on the issues you might have with finding the right job for you. Our officers are always ready to assist you on every kind of issue you might experience with job application process including masterclasses for polishing CVs and coverletters.

You can also use the services of JobShop via Career Connect. The link to Career Connect is available in MUSE, under "Services" Tab. The website allows to search for jobs, sign-up to Career events, book appointment with advisers and send queries.
