Welcome to SSiD

SSiD is the main resource of information on campus! It is also a place where a student can access the most important services, such as confirmation of student status, handling on enquiries just to name a few.

SSiD every day communicates thousands of general university enquiries, meets students and helps them to find an answer to their concerns.
